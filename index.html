The domDoodler

This program demos various DOM scripting to create a simple drawing program.

To draw, simply move your mouse in the screen. To change colors click on one of the colors in the top menu bar.

To toggle pen ON and OFF, just click the mouse, or click the 'Doodler' text in left area of the top menu bar.

Enjoy... try the Doodler Demo Now.
